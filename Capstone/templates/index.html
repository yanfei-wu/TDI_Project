People want ot cut down expensive electricity bill and want to protect themselves against unpredicted electricity rate rise. This can be one of the biggest motivations for people to choose solar energy.

Income Level

Although the price of solar has dropped significantly, the affordability is still a concern. In particularly, for people with low income and no financing, the expensive upfront cost for solar can be a deal killer.

Policies and Incentives

People are more likely to go solar if there are incentives such as tax credits for solar installation, or if there is policy to exempt them from paying property tax for the solar related home value increase.
